Hello!


My name is Jonathan Menschaert.
I am an aspiring game developer interested in the AI and other techniques used in games to create an adaptive experience.
My interests also go beyond the AI behaviors of npcs, I am fascinated by anything that is able adjust dynamically to the situation, and will gladly learn more about it and more!

Additionally, I also really like to build tools that can assist me in creating the games I want to make.
It can sometimes be very time-consuming to create things entirely by hand, especially in situations where there are specific needs for a project.
In such situations it is great to have specialized tools at hand that can automate a part of the process or at least make it easier.

Because I often experiment with the things mentioned above, I come into contact with a wide range of software and techniques,
which I sometimes add to my workflow for a project, so I consider myself a quick learner.
I also don't give up easily. I will always try to figure out a solution to a potential problem, and I am not afraid to explore different approaches if the situation asks for it.
