Hello! My name is Jonathan Menschaert. Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a big fan of adaptive gameplay, as it can create a unique experience for every player,
whether that happens through the AI of the npcs or through (strategic) choices the player makes.
It is fascinating to see the different ways players can interact or be interacted with in such a world.

I also like to build tools that can make it easier for me or assist me during the development process of a game! This can range from a tool that makes it easier to do certain things to one that can convert an input, such as json or other input format, to a specified output.
